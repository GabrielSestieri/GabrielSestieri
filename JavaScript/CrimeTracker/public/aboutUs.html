Our Project


Github Link

We are the Prince George’s County Crime Map, and our goal is to provide accurate and real time information for the residents of Prince George’s County, Maryland on the location of nearby vehicle crimes. We use a combination of the Prince George’s County API and the Google Maps API to make sure that our information is highly accurate and reliable, and in addition to this we allow residents to enter their own data related to the crimes for even faster updates to the situation than the police may be able to give themselves, all in order to achieve our desire of providing the best information about local crime that we can provide for the concerned citizens of Prince George’s County.
